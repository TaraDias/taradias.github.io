ABOUT
On the 10th of December 1996, a bored, heartbroken 19 year old college sophmore by the name of Duct Tape Boy, created a web zine known as +evolution+.


The e-zine explored a vast array of different topics that the sleep deprived teenager considered as important or relevant during the late 90s. Combined with poems, deep late night thoughts and his general concern for society, Duct Tape Boy released 12 issues withing the spam of only a couple of years.


The book, combines both opinions from the world that Duct Tape boy lived in (1990s) and the modern society that we live in today (2019). Art, music, youth culture, fashion, love, patriarchy and gender stereotypes are some of the few topics compared and contrasted from both decades in order to create an insight into what has changed and what has remained the same over the years.
